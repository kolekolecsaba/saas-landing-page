Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MailCommerce AI

Which e-commerce platforms do you integrate with?

MailCommerce AI integrates with all major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, and more. Our integration process is simple and only takes a few minutes to set up.

How does the AI create emails that match my brand?

When you integrate your store, our AI analyzes your brand elements, product catalog, and customer data to create a comprehensive brand DNA profile. This profile guides the AI in generating emails with copy and design that perfectly match your brand identity and business goals.

What types of automated email flows do you offer?

We offer all essential e-commerce email flows including welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, browse abandonment, post-purchase sequences, win-back campaigns, and more. You can set your flow requirements or let us build based on industry best practices.

How much time will I need to spend managing emails?

Very little! That's the beauty of MailCommerce AI. You'll just need to briefly answer a few key questions before the end of each month to guide your campaign calendar, and then approve the generated emails. Most customers spend less than 30 minutes per month on email marketing.

Can I edit the emails if I want to make changes?

Absolutely! If you don't like something, you can chat with our AI agent to change the structure of the proposed emails or adjust anything specific about the generated designs (copy/media/colors). Our AI will quickly implement your feedback and generate new versions.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans with no credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during your trial period to evaluate if MailCommerce AI is right for your e-commerce business.
